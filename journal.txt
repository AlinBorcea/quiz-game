1. Quiz Records
question,answers,correct
Quiz records are a trio of string, []string and int.
 - first element is the question, it must not be empty
 - second element is a string of answers separate by commas, there should be
at least two answers and each answer must have at least one character
 - third element is the index of the correct answer starting from 1
Records are validate only when needed. Make sure the csv file records are correct.
